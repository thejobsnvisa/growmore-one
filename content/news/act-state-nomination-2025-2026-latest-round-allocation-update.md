If you’re planning for ACT skilled migration, the latest 2025–2026 update is here. ACT released its most recent invitation round on **12 March 2026**, issuing nominations for both **Subclass 190** and **Subclass 491**.

Apart from this, the state has also shared how many nominations have been approved so far and how many spots are still available. This gives you a clearer picture of your chances and helps you plan your next steps effectively.
