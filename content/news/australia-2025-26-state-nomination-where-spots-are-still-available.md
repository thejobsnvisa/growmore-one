## Important Notes for Applicants

- Among all states and territories, **Queensland and Western Australia still have the highest remaining quotas for Subclass 190**, which gives skilled migrants a better chance if they meet the eligibility requirements.

- **Tasmania and the Northern Territory show the largest percentage of remaining Subclass 491** spots. However, for the Northern Territory, the application portal is currently closed, so only early submitted applications still have a chance to receive an invitation.

- Victoria shows a limited number of Subclass 491 spots left, so applicants targeting this state should act promptly or consider alternative pathways.

- New South Wales and South Australia have moderate remaining quotas for both Subclass 190 and Subclass 491, making them viable options for applicants who meet the state-specific eligibility criteria.
