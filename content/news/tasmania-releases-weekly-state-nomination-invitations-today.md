## Tasmania 26 Mar 2026 Skilled Migration Invitations
The Tasmanian has released its latest weekly state nomination invitations for skilled migration on 26 March 2026. It covers both Subclass 190 (Skilled Nominated Visa) and Subclass 491 (Skilled Work Regional Visa).

Although invitations are issued weekly, the current rounds are becoming especially important as Tasmania enters the final quarter of the migration year. It’s a crucial time for applicants aiming for state nomination, with competition getting tougher.

#### **Key Insights**

### **1\. Subclass 190 Highly Competitive**

*   Only **19 invitations were issued** in this round
*   Only **Gold Pass holders** were invited

### **2\. Subclass 491 Remains Active**

Tasmania continues to invite Green Pass applicants under subclass 491, but the competition is high. Candidates below 500 points may need to wait for the upcoming rounds or improve their eligibility.

### **3\. Nomination Places Are Filling Quickly**

As the places are filling quickly, make sure your ROI is up to date, you monitor weekly rounds for invitations, and maintain Tasmania-aligned employment and documentation. So, if you have already lodged an ROI, track the invitation rounds closely and be ready to submit a nomination application immediately if invited.

If you are planning to lodge an ROI, make sure your occupation matches Tasmania’s skilled migration requirements and take steps to maximise your points to improve your chances of receiving an invitation.
