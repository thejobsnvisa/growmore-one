## 2. Victoria Skilled Visa Program: Closing Soon

**URGENT:** The Victoria State Nomination Program for 2025–26 is approaching its final deadline. This is the last chance for applicants to be considered for this program year.

### 📅 Critical Deadline
* **ROI Submission Closure:** Tuesday, 28 April 2026 at 4:00 PM AEST.

### 📍 Allocation Overview
Victoria was allocated a total of **3,400 places**:
* **Subclass 190:** 2,700 places

**Note:** After the deadline, no new Registrations of Interest (ROIs) will be accepted. Assessment will continue only for those already in the system.


## 3. New Immigration Policy Proposals (Coalition)

The Coalition has introduced a proposal for a stricter migration framework. While these are **proposals only** and not currently law, they indicate potential future shifts.

### Proposed Highlights:
* **Legal Values Condition:** Making the Australian Values Statement a legally binding visa condition.
* **Social Media Vetting:** Increased screening of applicants' online activities.
* **Safe Countries List:** Restricting asylum applications from specific "safe" nations.
* **Temporary Protection Visas (TPVs):** Potential reintroduction of TPVs.

**Advice for Migrants:** There is no need to panic. Current visa rules remain unchanged, and applications are still being assessed under the existing framework.
