## ACT Canberra Matrix Final Round Expected in May 2026
The final countdown has begun for ACT skilled migration applicants, with the last Canberra Matrix invitation round for the 2025–26 financial year expected in May. As the program approaches its conclusion, candidates in the Canberra Matrix pool are entering an important phase.

So, applicants planning for ACT nomination should stay alert, as this period often brings key updates, limited invitations, and potential system changes.

**April Invitation Round Cancelled**
------------------------------------

The Australian Capital Territory (ACT) had earlier indicated a tentative 5th Canberra Matrix invitation round during the week of 13 April 2026. However, this date has now been removed from the schedule, and May is listed as the final round for the current financial year.

This means applicants who were expecting invitations in April to get an Australia PR will now need to prepare for the upcoming May round. With one confirmed round remaining, competition is expected to intensify among candidates.

**Limited Nomination Places Remaining**
---------------------------------------

Another key factor attracting attention is the remaining nomination allocation for the 2025–26 financial year.

Out of the total 1,600 nomination places allocated to ACT across [Skilled Nominated Visa](https://www.growmore.one/queensland-skilled-migration-program-2025-26-state-nomination-guide/) (Subclass 190) and Skilled Work Regional Visa ([Subclass 491](https://www.growmore.one/difference-between-subclass-491-and-subclass-190/)), only 639 nomination places remain available.

**May 2026: Final Invitation Round**
------------------------------------

The final Canberra Matrix invitation round is tentatively scheduled for the week of 4 May 2026, which marks the last invitation opportunity for this financial year.

Applicants with strong Canberra Matrix scores, relevant employment, and competitive profiles are more likely to receive invitations during this final stage.

**Additional Invitations May Be Very Limited**
----------------------------------------------

ACT has also indicated that additional invitations may be issued after the May round if any nomination allocations remain unused. However:

*   Invitation numbers are expected to be very limited
*   Availability will depend on remaining allocations
*   Not all occupations may receive additional invitations

Applicants should therefore consider the May round as the final major opportunity for ACT nomination this financial year.

**New Canberra Matrix Portal Expected**
---------------------------------------

Adding to the update, ACT has also indicated that a new Canberra Matrix portal is expected to launch in May. This may introduce:

*   System updates
*   Process changes
*   Possible scoring or eligibility adjustments

Applicants should ensure their profiles remain updated and accurate ahead of these potential changes.

**Plan Your ACT Nomination Strategy Before May**
------------------------------------------------

With the final Canberra Matrix invitation round approaching and limited nomination places remaining, this is a critical time for applicants who are aiming for ACT nomination. Working with an experienced Skilled migration consultant Australia can help you assess your eligibility, strengthen your Canberra Matrix profile, and improve your chances in this competitive final round.

If ACT invitations become limited, you can also identify alternative skilled migration pathways, including other state nominations or visa options. This may help you to keep your [Australia PR](https://www.growmore.one/) plans on track.
