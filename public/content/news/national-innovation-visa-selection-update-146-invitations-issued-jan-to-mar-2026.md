## What is the National Innovation Visa (Subclass 858)?

The National Innovation Visa (subclass 858) is an Australia PR visa designed for individuals who are not just skilled but recognised for delivering exceptional results in their field. It targets individuals who can make a real economic or innovation-driven impact in Australia, particularly in high-growth and future-focused sectors.

**It includes:**

* Global researchers driving impactful work
* Entrepreneurs building or scaling ventures
* Innovative investors investing in high-value ideas
* Athletes and creatives with recognised achievements

To apply under this subclass, applicants must go through the Australia EOI process by highlighting their achievements and impact. Only candidates who receive an invitation can move forward with the visa application.

## What This Round Really Shows

**Tech & Healthcare Are Leading:** Fields like Artificial Intelligence, critical technologies, and healthcare dominated invitations that indicate Australia is currently prioritising high-impact and future-focused sectors.

**Strong Profiles Are Winning:** The majority of invitations issued under Priority Three highlight that measurable, credible achievements are central to selection.

**Only a Small Number Get Selected:** Just 146 out of 1,815 EOIs received invitations, which indicates that to stand out under this visa subclass, applicants must possess a clearly differentiated profile.

**How You Present Your EOI Matters:** Strong achievements alone are not enough, but candidates should clearly demonstrate their impact and alignment with priority sectors.
