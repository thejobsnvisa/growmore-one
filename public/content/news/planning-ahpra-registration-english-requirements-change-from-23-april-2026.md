There is a significant update for those planning AHPRA registration, as the authority has introduced new English language score requirements, which will come into effect from **23 April 2026**.

While some overall score requirements and sections like Listening and Reading have been reduced, speaking requirements have increased across multiple tests. This means achieving strong communication skills is now even more important for meeting AHPRA registration requirements.


## Old vs New AHPRA English Score Requirements

### OET Score Changes
| Test | Listening | Reading | Writing | Speaking |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Before 23 April 2026** | B | B | C+ | B |
| **After 23 April 2026** | 350 | 360 | 350 | 360 |

This update converts grade-based OET scores into numeric score requirements, which means a more standardised scoring format across English tests.

### PTE Academic Score Changes
| Test | Listening | Reading | Writing | Speaking | Overall |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Before 23 April 2026** | 66 | 66 | 56 | 66 | 66 |
| **After 23 April 2026** | 58 | 59 | 60 | 76 | 63 |

In these changes, listening and reading score requirements have been reduced while writing and speaking requirements have increased. Here, focus clearly shifts toward the speaking part, which makes it the most important component in the PTE test.

### TOEFL iBT Score Changes
| Test | Listening | Reading | Writing | Speaking | Overall |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Before 23 April 2026** | 24 | 24 | 24 | 23 | 94 |
| **After 23 April 2026** | 22 | 22 | 23 | 24 | 91 |

The overall TOEFL iBT score requirement has been reduced, including Listening, Reading, and Writing, while the Speaking requirement has increased. This means speaking now plays a more important role in meeting the English language requirement.

### Cambridge C1 Advanced Changes
| Test | Listening | Reading | Writing | Speaking | Overall |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Before 23 April 2026** | 185 | 185 | 176 | 185 | 185 |
| **After 23 April 2026** | 175 | 179 | 180 | 194 | 178 |

Speaking requirements have increased significantly in Cambridge C1 Advanced, while Writing has also increased and overall scores have been reduced.

### Cambridge C2 Proficiency (No Change)

### IELTS Academic (No Change)

## Planning AHPRA Registration?

Meeting English requirements is only one part of the AHPRA registration process for internationally qualified professionals planning to work in Australia. There are several other important factors you should plan for early, especially if your long-term goal is Australia PR.

It includes:
* Choosing the right AHPRA registration pathway
* Preparing the required documents for assessment
* Completing skills assessment (if applicable)
* Exploring suitable visa options for working in Australia
* Understanding potential employer sponsorship opportunities

Getting the right advice early can help you avoid delays and make your journey smoother. Especially if your goal is to work in Australia’s healthcare sector, then speaking with a MARA registered agent in Australia can help you understand your options clearly, identify the right pathway, and plan your next steps with confidence.
