## What It Means for Applicants

A closer look at the 30 April round against the previous round data gives a more accurate picture of where Tasmania’s nomination program is heading. 

Total invitations have increased from 42 to 64, and Subclass 190 shifted from a strict 1000 (Gold Pass only) to 352 (Green Pass). At the same time, Subclass 491 has only marginally changed from 57 to 56 points, which tells you one thing: the selection threshold is holding steady and not dropping.

## How to Strengthen Your Chances
