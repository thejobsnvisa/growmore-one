# Australia Opens Subclass 192 Tuvalu Ballot on 1 May 2026 With 280 Places

Good news for Tuvalu citizens who are considering a move to Australia. The Department of Home Affairs has announced the opening of the **Pacific Engagement Visa (Subclass 192) – Treaty Stream ballot** from **1 May 2026 to 1 June 2026**.

A total of **280 Treaty stream visa places** are available for the **2026–27 program year**.

This **Falepili Mobility Pathway** offers eligible Tuvalu citizens an opportunity to become permanent residents of Australia through a **random ballot selection process**.

Unlike many other Australian migration pathways, the Subclass 192 Treaty Stream provides significant flexibility and allows successful applicants to:

- Live anywhere in Australia  
- Work without restrictions  
- Study freely  

There are **no minimum skill level requirements, occupation restrictions, or English language requirements**.

## How the Subclass 192 Tuvalu Ballot Works

The process involves two main steps:

### Step 1: Ballot Registration
Eligible Tuvalu citizens must register for the ballot between **1 May 2026 and 1 June 2026**.

### Step 2: Random Selection
After the ballot closes, the Department of Home Affairs will randomly select applicants. Selected candidates will then be invited to submit a visa application.

The selection process is expected to take place shortly after the ballot closes.

## Eligibility Requirements

To register for the Subclass 192 Treaty Stream (Tuvalu) ballot, applicants must:

- Be at least **18 years old** when the ballot opens  
- Be a **citizen of Tuvalu** (not obtained through investment)  
- Hold a **valid Tuvalu passport**  
- Be **born in Tuvalu**, or have a parent or grandparent born in Tuvalu  
- Not be a **New Zealand citizen**  
- Not already be registered in the same ballot  
- Pay the **AUD 25 ballot registration fee**  

Applicants can register from **inside or outside Australia**.

## Why This Visa Stands Out

The **Pacific Engagement Visa (Subclass 192)** is unique because it offers an alternative to skilled and employer-sponsored visas, with:

- Direct **permanent residency**  
- No **occupation restrictions**  
- No **employer sponsorship required**  
- No **English test requirement**  
- No **points-based competition**  

However, with only **280 places available**, early preparation and accurate registration are essential.

## What This Means for Applicants

This visa works like a rare **Australia PR lottery**. If selected, you get a direct opportunity to apply for permanent residency through a random process.

However, applicants must still:

- Meet all eligibility criteria  
- Be ready to lodge their application within the given timeframe
