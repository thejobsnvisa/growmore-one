As announced earlier by the state, the latest NSW Subclass 491 Pathway 2 invitation round results have now been released on 30 April 2026. 

The results make one thing very clear: NSW is no longer relying on a simple points system. Instead, invitations are being issued based on a combination of which occupations are in demand, where the applicant is based, and how closely they connect to the real job market needs in NSW.

This round is heavily dominated by onshore invitations, which shows a clear preference for candidates already living and working in Australia.

## Key Insight from This Round

* Plumbers and Roof Plumbers are getting invites at just 65 points, which seems the lowest in this round. So, for onshore tradespeople, this looks like a more accessible pathway right now.
* Systems Administrators, ICT Security Specialists, and Network Engineers are at 115 points. These are not just high-score profiles, but they are built with strong combinations like Australian experience, NAATI, English, and age points.
* Even within the same occupation, outcomes differ based on experience, partner points, and whether the applicant is onshore or offshore. 
* Secondary Teachers are at 100 points, which is higher than Primary Teachers at 90 points.
